Perks

Perks are traits that are essential to surviving a trial. Each character is provided with 3 perks special to their character. These perks can be shared with other characters you own when the character that has the traits reaches Prestige 1. I will not list all the perks, as there are 11 pages of perks that can be bought through the blood web, but I will list the specific perks for Trevor Belmont and Aestri Yazar, and the perks that I use for Aestri in my own gameplay.
A character can use up to 4 perks. When using a characater for the first time, you can use 1 perk. As you advance through levels in the blood web, you will unlock more perk slots, and you can use more perks that are in your perks inventory.

Aestri Yazar

Aestri Yazar comes with 3 perks that are specific to her character:

Bardic Inspiration
Rolls a D20. Various intervals determine the repair boost of generators for survivors in a 16 meter radius, and lasts for 90 seconds
Mirrored Illusion
After 20% total generator repairs, you can spawn a Static Illusion next to chests, generators, or totems for 40-60 seconds.
Still Sight
After standing still for 4-6 seconds, you can see the aura of the killer, generators, and chests in a 24 meter radius until you move again.



Here are the perks that I use for Aestri Yazar in my gameplay:

Technician (Feng Min)
Noises caused by reparis are reduced by 8 meters, failing a skill check will cause the following:
    • Generator explosion will be prevented.
    • The generator looses progress as usual.
    • An additional 3% progress is lost.
Poised (Jane Romero)
When first starting repairs on a generator, the Killer's aura is revealead for 6 seconds. When completing a generator, you will not leave scratch marks for 14 seconds.
Bardic Inspiration (Aestri Yazar)
Press and hold the Ability Button 1 while standing motionless to enter the performance interaction, lasting 15 seconds, and empowers survivors in a 16 meter radius.
It has a 90 second cooldown. Roll a 20, and the effect lasts for 90 seconds if the performance is completed:
    • 1 | You scream and nothing happens.
    • 2-10 | Skill Checks give +1% progress.
    • 11-19 | Skill Checks give +2% progress.
    • 20 | Skill checks give +3% progress.
Iron Will (Jake Park)
Grunts of pain caused by injuries are reduced by 100%. Doesn't function while Exhaused. Does not inflict Exhausted.


Trevor Belmont

Trevor Belmont comes with 3 perks that are specific to his character:

Exultation
Stunning the killer with a pallet upgrades the rarity of your held item to the next tier. Recharges the item by 25%.
Eyes of Belmont
When a generator is completed, the aura of the killer is revealed to you for 1-3 seconds.
Moment of Glory
After performing the Unlocking or Rummaging action on chests a total of 2 times, Moment of Glory activates:
    • When you become injured, you instantly suffer from the Broken Status Effect
    • You are automatically healed 1 Health State after 60-80 seconds
      • This effect is cancelled prematurely if you enter the Dying State.
